Game Rules

Controls

  • ← → Move piece left/right

Game Objective

  • Fill a complete row to clear it
  • Clear multiple rows simultaneously for bonus points
  • Speed increases as level goes up
  • Game ends when pieces reach the top

Scoring

  • Clear 1 line: 100 points
  • Clear 2 lines: 300 points
  • Clear 3 lines: 500 points
  • Clear 4 lines: 800 points (Tetris!)
